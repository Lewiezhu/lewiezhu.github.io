FAQ

Q:How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

A:By making use of the hz student pass on a nearby printer and use the website print.hz.nl

Q:How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

A:Go to one of the printers that are placed around the school and send it to your student mail.

Q:How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event at Friday 16/09) on the HZ web shop?

A:Login on the web shop and order it.

Q:Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?

A: Visit the webpage of Hz about coronavirus

Q:How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

A:Make a reservation for a available timeslot and room through the online environment

Q:What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

A:Students and staff can only enter by presenting their HZ pass at the barriers, guests can ring the bell at the barrier.
