Veolia Environnement

Veolia environnement is a french multinational company that is offering services in the fields of water management, wast management and energy services. The company has around 180.000 employees in their organisation and is active in 48 different countries. The company has a wide arrange of engineers,consultants and developers that specialise in creating solutions or managing in their active fields.

Internally, Veolia priorities is to develop its employees skills and protect their health and safety at work. One of their job descriptions is to create cloud based solutions for projects.

Its quite interesting how internationally active they are and on such a big scale.
